We are the Georgia Tech Visual Analytics Lab.

Our Mission

The goal of our research is to develop interactive visual analytic applications that help people make sense of data. We approach this challenge through combining scientific techniques from:

  • information visualization,
  • machine learning,
  • data mining,
  • and human-computer interaction

to produce usable and powerful visual analytics applications.
